Audio Engineering

From laying tracks to flying hooks, Casey specializes in vocal production, mixing dialogue for podcasts, radio and film; producing modern hip-hop and rap vocals, layered harmonies; and everything else.


In addition to studio engineering, Casey also offers location sound, live-sound engineering, post-production editing and mixing, foley recording, ADR, sound design, front-of-house engineering, monitor mixing, audio restoration, and broadcast mixing services.


  1. Engineering

    Basic tracking. Record vocals, dialogue, voice-overs, instruments...

  2. Editing

    Audio cleanup. Cut for time, content, and clarity. Repair low-quality recordings, reduce noise, eliminate clicks, pops, etc...

  3. Mixing

    Mix vocals, ad-libs, doubles, harmonies, ƒx… to stereo or multi-tracks audio files. Add delays, reverb, pitch-shifting, doubling, and more.

  4. Mastering

    Optimized LUFS for streaming. High-resolution deliverables. Singles mastering or whole projects. Stem or stereo-track.
