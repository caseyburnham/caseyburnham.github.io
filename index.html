Casey Burnham,
Mix Engineer

Casey is an accomplished Sound & Mix Engineer with a B.S. in Recording Arts from UCD. From laying tracks to flying hooks, Casey specializes in vocal production, mixing dialogue for podcasts, radio and film; producing modern hip-hop and rap vocals, layered harmonies; and everything else. Based in Colorado, Casey primarily does his engineering, mixing, and mastering out of The Lab Recording Studios in Denver.
