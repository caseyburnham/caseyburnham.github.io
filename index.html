About Casey - Audio Engineer in Denver, Colorado

A former Idahoan who traded potato fields for mountain trails, I moved to Colorado in 2011 and earned a degree in Audio Engineering from UCD. Currently serving as Audio Director for the Arvada Center, I've been a full-time live sound and theater engineer for over a decade ‚Äî wrangling mics, troubleshooting gear that swore it was working yesterday, and convincing everyone it was fine all along.

Skills & Experience

Live Sound Engineering & Theater Audio

  • FOH and live stream mixing for large-scale musicals, concerts, and other events.

Audio System Design & Integration
