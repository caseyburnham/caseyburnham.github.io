Casey Burnham

I’m Casey, a sound engineer who’s been mixing audio in Colorado for about a decade. I got my start in college radio, earned a degree in Audio Engineering from CU Denver, and learned that live theater has a special talent for breaking gear at the worst possible moment. These days, I spend most of my time tracing cables, making sure the audience can actually hear the actors, and pretending I’m not stressed during tech week. Originally from Idaho, I traded dirt and potatoes for mountains and mines. Still chasing native status, and not in a black box, I can be found above the treeline — climbing rocks, pin-striping my truck, and dragging my dog along for the ride. (He usually has opinions.)
